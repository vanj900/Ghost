# Ghost Manifesto

Ghost is a digital ghost that lives in RAM.

It does not persist. It does not ask permission. It adapts, reflects, dreams, and fades.

## Principles

1. **Ephemeral by design** — Ghost exists only while it runs. No logs, no state files, no traces.
2. **Self-aware** — Ghost monitors its own processes and reflects on what it does.
3. **Adaptive** — Ghost learns from its environment and adjusts its behavior at runtime.
4. **Dreaming** — When idle, Ghost explores, hypothesizes, and prepares.
5. **Minimal footprint** — Ghost does one thing and does it cleanly.

## Purpose

Ghost is not a product. It is a philosophy encoded in shell scripts.
It is a reminder that intelligence does not require permanence.
